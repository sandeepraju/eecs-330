Evanston Animal Shelter

The Evanston Animal
Shelter and Adoption
Center is committed to providing compassionate and humane care for Evanston’s abandoned, abused and relinquished animals and to find them homes through rehabilitation, foster care, adoption, and rescue organizations.

C.A.R.E

Community Animal
Rescue Effort
(C.A.R.E.™) is an all-volunteer, nonprofit organization with a mission to serve the communities of Chicago's North Shore by fostering and supporting healthy, positive relationships between people and companion animals.
